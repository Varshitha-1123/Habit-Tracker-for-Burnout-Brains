Recovery Tips

Gentle strategies for burnout recovery

Prioritize Rest

Allow yourself to rest without guilt. Your body needs downtime to recover from burnout. Try a 20-minute power nap or simply lie down with no agenda.

Gentle Movement

Instead of intense workouts, try gentle walks, stretching, or yoga. Movement helps release endorphins without adding stress to your body.

Mindfulness Moments

Practice 5-minute mindfulness exercises. Focus on your breath or observe your surroundings without judgment. It helps calm an overactive mind.

Nourish Gently

Focus on simple, nourishing meals. Don't stress about perfect nutrition. What matters is eating regularly and staying hydrated.

Set Boundaries

Learn to say no without guilt. Protect your energy by setting clear boundaries around work, social commitments, and screen time.
